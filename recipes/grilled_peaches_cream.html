Grilled Peaches and Cream



This is an easy grilled peach dessert! I use a honey nut flavored cream cheese spread and it's wonderful! Drizzle a little extra honey after they're grilled and they're perfect!

Ingredient

  • 4 peaches, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons clover honey
  • 1 cup soft cream cheese with honey and nuts
  • 1 cup soft cream cheese with honey and nuts

How to make it

  1. Preheat a grill for medium-high heat.
  2. Brush peaches with a light coating of oil.
  3. Place pit side down onto the grill. Grill for 5 minutes, or until the surfaces have nice grill marks.
  4. Turn the peaches over, and drizzle with a bit of honey.
  5. Place a dollop of the cream cheese spread in the place where the pit was.
  6. Grill for 2 to 3 more minutes, or until the filling is warm. Serve immediately.
